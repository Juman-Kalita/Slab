## Features Included

### Employee Management
- Add / edit / deactivate employees
- Employee profiles with photo, contact, department, designation
- Role-based access (Admin, Manager, Employee)

### Attendance Tracking
- Daily clock-in / clock-out with timestamps
- Late arrival and early departure detection
- Monthly attendance summary per employee
- Leave request and approval system
- Holiday calendar management

### Task Management
- Create and assign tasks to employees or teams
- Set deadlines and priority levels
- Track task status (Pending / In Progress / Completed)
- Manager can view all team tasks in one place

### Admin Dashboard
- Overview of total employees, present/absent today
- Department-wise attendance stats
- Recent activity log
- Quick actions panel

### Reports & Export
- Monthly attendance reports (PDF / Excel)
- Employee-wise productivity summary
- Leave balance reports
- Payroll-ready attendance data export

### Security & Access
- Secure login with encrypted session management
- Role-based permissions
- Full activity audit log
- Data encrypted at rest and in transit
